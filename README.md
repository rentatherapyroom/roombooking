## ðŸ§  About CBT

Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT) is a structured, evidence-based form of psychological treatment used to help individuals understand the relationship between thoughts, emotions, and behaviours. CBT is commonly used to treat:

- Anxiety disorders  
- OCD  
- Depression  
- Health anxiety  
- Social anxiety  
- Panic disorder  
- Phobias  
- General stress and worry  

CBT is most effective when delivered by a trained, accredited therapist who uses collaboratively developed treatment plans and measurable progress tracking.
